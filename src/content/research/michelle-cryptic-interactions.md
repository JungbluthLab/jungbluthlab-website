I am very interested in the cryptic interactions that occur in marine plankton. Many of these interactions — from predation to parasitism to mutualism — are hidden in plain sight. Understanding these hidden but important ecological relationships is essential for a complete picture of marine food web dynamics and ecosystem functioning.
